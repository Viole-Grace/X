Project X

The Pauper's CamScanner


Why 'Webapp'

CamScanner, along with a few other apps have been banned in India following skirmishes between India and China. As the application is extremely useful, we decided to make a base version of the app. This WebApp is to serve as a Proof of Concept for the application we have made, project X.
The project basis is to emulate simple CamScanner functionality - upload images, automatically select target area and then convert it to a PDF format.
